Academics



Communication Skills

“At Good Children High School, communication skills are a top priority. From primary classes, students are encouraged to speak in English, fostering fluency and confidence. Interactive activities, debates, and presentations help enhance their verbal and written abilities, preparing them for global opportunities. The supportive environment ensures every student develops strong, effective communication skills.”

English Lab

“In our School we have English labs which are vibrant centers for language learning. Starting from primary classes, students are encouraged to speak in English, enhancing their fluency and confidence. Equipped with interactive tools and resources, these labs provide immersive experiences in reading, writing, and speaking, ensuring students develop strong communication skills for future success.”

Computer Lab

“The Computer Lab at Our School is a state-of-the-art facility designed to educate students on the latest technological advancements. Equipped with modern computers, software, and high-speed internet, it provides hands-on experience in coding, digital literacy, and multimedia projects. Our dedicated instructors guide students through various computer skills, preparing them for a technology-driven future and enhancing their problem-solving and critical thinking abilities. The lab fosters a collaborative learning environment, encouraging innovation and creativity.”

IIT JEE Foundation

“At our school, we are committed to academic excellence and future success. To support this, we offer an IIT-JEE foundation program starting from class 6. This early preparation helps students build a strong foundation in mathematics, science, and analytical skills, setting them on the path to success in one of India’s most competitive engineering entrance exams. Our dedicated faculty and structured curriculum ensure comprehensive and focused learning, giving our students a significant advantage in their academic journey.”

Digital Classrooms

“At our school, every classroom is equipped with projectors, promoting a dynamic digital education experience. These digital classes transform traditional learning, making lessons more interactive and engaging. Through multimedia presentations, educational videos, and interactive content, students gain a deeper understanding of subjects. This technology-driven approach enhances the learning environment, fosters creativity, and prepares students for a digital future.”

Badminton Court

“The badminton court beside the School is a vibrant space dedicated to encouraging students in sports. It provides an excellent opportunity for students to stay active, develop their physical skills, and foster teamwork. With regular coaching sessions and friendly matches, the court helps nurture young talents, promoting a healthy lifestyle and love for sports among students. Additionally, it is open to outsiders during designated playing hours, fostering community engagement and providing everyone with the chance to enjoy the sport.”

Abacus & Vedic Maths

“At our school, we integrate Vedic Math and Abacus training into our curriculum to enhance students’ mathematical skills. Vedic Math techniques simplify complex calculations, promote mental agility, and faster problem-solving. Abacus training improves concentration, memory, and numerical understanding. These innovative methods make learning math fun and effective, empowering students with strong analytical and cognitive abilities from an early age.”

Seminars

“At our school, we conduct monthly seminars to encourage students in stage performing. These seminars provide a platform for students to showcase their talents, develop public speaking skills, and build confidence. Through presentations, debates, and performances, students learn to express themselves effectively and overcome stage fright. This regular exposure to public speaking and performance arts fosters a supportive environment where students can grow and excel in their communication and presentation abilities.”
