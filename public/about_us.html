About Us




PRINCIPAL WELCOMES YOU
C. Pavan Kumar

I am delighted to offer you a warm welcome to Good Children English Medium School. I am personally very proud of this school and the all-round, inclusive, and holistic education you will find here.

Offering a broad, inclusive education has been a core component of Good Children English Medium School's ethics since our founding. Our school has always set out to bring out the best in every child in our care, and we are determined to continue to nurture this culture today.

We have high expectations at Good Children English Medium School, and this encourages our pupils to believe in themselves, be independent, be resilient, and show ambition both in and out of the classroom. Our pupils feel happy and safe to learn, which leads to them achieving fantastic exam results, and social awareness for lifelong learning needed to succeed in a fast-changing world.

We value respect for tradition and an openness to new ideas. We encourage our pupils to work with and learn from each other while aspiring to the courage to stand up for what they believe in, as important that we can live alongside with develop all those who have the fortune to walk through our gates, whether those who arrived who need a helping hand.

Our aim is to send our pupils out into the world with strong friendships already forged and the ability to make new ones, with the knowledge and skills to answer universally with empathy, and to bring a more inspired generation into this world.

I hope that you will be thrilled with our helpful and that it offers a glimpse of what our school is all about. First and foremost, I wish to wish you the child that it has been a gift to care for the best and proud to help provide what Good Children English Medium School is all about is to see it for yourself.

I do hope you will come and visit the school, see first-hand what Good Children English Medium School does each and every day, and find out what it could do for you, too.

C. Pavan Kumar
Principal
