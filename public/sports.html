  • Kho Kho

    Kho-Kho is a traditional Indian sport played by two teams of twelve players each, nine of whom enter the field. It involves chasing and tagging opponents across the court, emphasizing speed, agility, and teamwork. Players take turns to run and dodge while the opposing team attempts to tag them out.

  • Kabaddi

    Kabaddi is a contact sport originating from India, featuring two teams of seven players. The game combines elements of wrestling and tag, where a "raider" enters the opposing team's half to tag players while holding their breath, chanting "kabaddi." Success requires strength, agility, and tactical acumen.
